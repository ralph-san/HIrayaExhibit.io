Shibuya Crossing is best known for the incredible 'scramble' that occurs every time the traffic lights turn red, stopping all vehicles in every direction to allow a huge wave of pedestrians to flood into the intersection for a few moments. It is located in front of Shibuya Station where you can also see the Hachikō Memorial Statue.

ALWAYS IN THE RUSH: BUSY CITY

Dana Cruz

Here and there go and stop what a busy day on weekdays!


After the pandemic, cars emerged causing traffic jams. Traffic is always a problem in the city mostly in Quezon City. Every day, we have experience traffic so much that we are late in schools or planned events. But neither of us all wished for it to happen.


These traffic jams mostly happen because of road construction and accidents. There are some solutions to reduce the traffic. First is to provide proper lanes for vehicles and use new innovative ideas on public transport.


it is not really simple to decrease traffic congestion. Hence, the government must focus on this problem first to the welfare of the people.
