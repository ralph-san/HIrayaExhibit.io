URBAN

Urban areas are densely populated areas characterized by buildings, streets, and infrastructure. They are often defined by their physical and social characteristics, such as a diverse population and a high level of economic activity, and can offer opportunities for work and cultural participation. However, urban areas can also have challenges such as congestion, pollution, and a lack of affordable housing. They are often contrasted with rural areas, which have a lower population density and a more agricultural or natural landscape.
